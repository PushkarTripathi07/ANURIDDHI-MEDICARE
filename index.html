Core Values

Integrity, Empathy, Quality First, Customer-Centric Approach, and Continuous Innovation in healthcare.

Anuriddhi Medicare Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2025 with a clear vision of improving access to quality medicines and healthcare solutions. Since its foundation, the organization has been committed to serving society with trust, care, and responsibility. Built on strong ethical values and customer-first thinking, Anuriddhi Medicare aims to become a reliable name in the medical and healthcare sector.

The company is proudly led by its directors, Tushar Kant Tripathi and Pushkar Tripathi, who bring forward a shared vision of innovation, service, and excellence. Their leadership focuses on creating a system where quality healthcare products are easily accessible and affordable to people from all sections of society. With a deep understanding of healthcare needs, they continue to guide the organization toward sustainable growth and impactful service.

At Anuriddhi Medicare, our core belief is reflected in our motto, “We Care with Medicine.” This is not just a statement, but a promise that drives every aspect of our work. From sourcing genuine pharmaceutical products to ensuring proper distribution and customer satisfaction, we maintain strict quality standards and ethical practices. Our goal is to support better health outcomes by providing reliable medicines and healthcare essentials.

Customer trust is the foundation of our success. We believe in building long-term relationships by delivering consistent quality, transparent services, and responsive support. With a strong foundation, visionary leadership, and a clear mission, Anuriddhi Medicare continues its journey toward excellence—delivering care, building trust, and making healthcare better for everyone.
